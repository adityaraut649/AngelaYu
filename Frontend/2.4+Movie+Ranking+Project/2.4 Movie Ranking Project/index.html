The Best Movies According to Angela Yu

My top 3 Movies of all time

The Matrix

The Matrix is a science fiction action film released in 1999 by the Wachowskis. It is the first installment in the Matrix film series. The film is based on the 1974 science fiction novel The Matrix by Donald E. Knuth and published by the Wachowskis. The film was directed by Andy Wachowski and stars Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss, Hugo Weaving, and Joel Silver. The film follows the story of a computer hacker who discovers that the world he knows is a simulated reality created by a computer program. He is then forced to fight against the program and its creator, Neo, to save the world.
